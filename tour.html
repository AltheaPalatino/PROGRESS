Almaty & Shymbulak Ski Resort

Almaty is the heart of Kazakhstan’s culture and entertainment, with beautiful parks, museums, and a vibrant city life. Shymbulak Ski Resort offers breathtaking mountain views, world-class skiing in winter, and scenic hiking in summer.

Charyn Canyon

This natural wonder, often called the “Grand Canyon of Kazakhstan,” features stunning red rock formations shaped by nature over millions of years. It's perfect for hiking, photography, and experiencing Kazakhstan’s wild beauty.

Astana (Nur-Sultan)

The futuristic capital is home to incredible modern architecture like Baiterek Tower and Khan Shatyr. It’s a unique blend of innovation and Kazakh culture, making it a must-see for architecture lovers.

Lake Kaindy & Kolsai Lakes

Lake Kaindy’s eerie submerged forest and the breathtaking Kolsai Lakes make this one of the most beautiful natural spots in Kazakhstan. It’s a paradise for hikers, photographers, and nature enthusiasts.

Medeu Ice Rink

The world’s highest-altitude ice skating rink, nestled in the mountains near Almaty, offers a unique skating experience. It’s a must-visit for winter sports enthusiasts and those who love a scenic atmosphere.
