Top Leisure Activities in Kazakhstan

Hiking in the Tian Shan Mountains

Explore breathtaking mountain trails, such as those in Almaty’s Ile-Alatau National Park and around the stunning Big Almaty Lake.

Skiing and Snowboarding in Shymbulak

Located near Almaty, Shymbulak Ski Resort offers top-tier skiing and snowboarding experiences with modern facilities.

Visiting Charyn Canyon

This natural wonder of Charyn Canyon is perfect for hiking, photography, and camping.

Exploring the Kolsai and Kaindy Lakes

These beautiful alpine lakes provide opportunities for boating, fishing, and picnicking amidst stunning scenery.

Riding Horses on the Steppe

Experience Kazakhstan’s nomadic heritage by riding horses across the vast open landscapes of the steppe.

Relaxing at Borovoe (Burabay) Resort

A picturesque area with lakes, pine forests, and fresh air, ideal for hiking, swimming, and relaxation.

Sandboarding in the Singing Dunes

Located in Altyn-Emel National Park, these unique dunes create a humming sound when the wind blows, and they are great for sandboarding or sightseeing.

Visiting Traditional Kazakh Bazaars

Enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of markets like Green Bazaar in Almaty, where you can taste local delicacies and shop for souvenirs.

Eagle Hunting in Nura or Almaty Region

Experience the ancient tradition of hunting with golden eagles, where skilled Kazakh hunters (berkutchi) demonstrate their incredible bond with these majestic birds.
